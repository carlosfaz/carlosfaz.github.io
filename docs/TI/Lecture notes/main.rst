Other good Books and review papers on this subject:

- `Topological Insulators and Topological Superconductors by Bernevig, B. <http://www.amazon.com/Topological-Insulators-Superconductors-Andrei-Bernevig/dp/069115175X>`_.
- `Topological Insulators: Dirac Equation in Condensed Matters by Shun-Qing Shen <http://www.amazon.com/Topological-Insulators-Equation-Condensed-Solid-State/dp/3642328571/ref=sr_1_2?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1415444346&sr=1-2&keywords=Topological+Insulators>`_.
- M. Fruchart and D. Carpentier, Comptes Rendus Physique 14, 779 (2013).
- M. Kohmoto, Annals of Physics 160, 343 (1985).
- D. Xiao, M.-C. Chang, and Q. Niu, Reviews of Modern Physics 82, 1959 (2010).
- Y. Mokrousov and F. Freimuth, arXiv:1407.2847 [cond-Mat] (2014).

More geometrical staffs at this one `Geometry, Topology and Physics, by Nakahara <http://www.amazon.com/Geometry-Topology-Physics-Edition-Graduate/dp/0750306068>`_.
